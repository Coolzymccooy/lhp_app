About Us

Discover Our Story

WELCOME

RCCG LIGHTHOUSE PARISH is a place where people can meet Jesus, engage in life-giving community. We are the space where people can have authentic encounters with Christ, discover their gifts and use them for God's glory. You are very welcome, but much more than that, you are indeed wanted and encouraged to stay and belong. Join us for any of our Sunday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday or Friday services!

VISION

  • To make heaven, not compromising holiness.
  • To take as many people along.
  • To raise leaders who would fulfill:
    • Their callings and
    • The vision of exceeding greatness.
  • To build a people manifesting the spirit of Love, Excellence, Faithfulness, Character and Integrity, who will do the following:
    • Evangelize and positively impact the world.
    • Establish God's government, order and structure.
    • Demonstrate the relevance of Christianity through contemporary and creative expression of our faith.

WHAT WE OFFER

Supporting Our Community

Food Bank

We believe that no child & adult should go to bed hungry. The church provides non-perishable foods to members and anyone from the public. Just contact us on any of the above numbers and we will arrange collection or drop off.

Free Food & Snacks

This is provided on Sundays and during the week on request. Contact the above numbers for information about free food which is usually available all Sunday afternoons. Free snacks and hot/cold drinks are available every Sunday AM and PM. We can arrange fresh food if you contact us.

Car Wash Vouchers

Available on request from the church.

How We Support & Integrate Within The Community

Pastoral & Welfare Support

Available both face to face and over the telephone. Please contact us for details.

Practical Assistance & Advice with Settling into the UK

We provide this to new families within the community. Please get in touch for advice about obtaining accommodation, homelessness assistance, job applications, linking up with services, education, shopping for world foods and anything you require advice and assistance with especially if you're new in the local community and we'll be glad to help you settle into your new environment.

Parenting & Advice on Marital Issues

We offer free parenting classes to members and have programs and seminars on marriage. This runs during the year. Please contact us if you're interested.

Women & Men's Ministry

We reach out to both men & women by organising programs and seminars to meet their different needs. Our Women's ministry is called Women of Worth which is a Community of ladies who love the Lord and belong to a community. They have fellowship and prayers meeting weekly. Our men's ministry is called Men of Valour. Our men meet together to discuss important matters.

Children's Church

We run sessions for children on some Sundays where children learn in their own space and pace. This is supervised and led by trusted adults who have been DBS checked and trained.

Summer Mentoring Classes

For girls, comes up in August.
