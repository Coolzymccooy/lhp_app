FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

What are your service times?

We hold multiple services throughout the week:
Sundays: Sunrise Service (7:30am), Sunshine Service (10:30am)
Wednesdays: Carpe Diem (6:30am)
Thursdays: Open Heavens (6:00pm)
Fridays: Praise Hour (12:00pm)

Where is the church located?

We are located at The Rock Shopping Centre, Vue Cinema, The Rock, Bury, BL9 OND, Manchester, United Kingdom.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there are parking space available at Argos.

What should I wear to church?

Come as you are! We want you to feel comfortable. Most people dress in smart casual attire, but there's no dress code.

Can I watch services online?

Yes! We live stream all our services on YouTube and our website. Click "WATCH LIVE SERVICES" in the navigation menu to join us online.

Is there a children's program?

Yes, we have age-appropriate programs for children from infants to 12 years old during our Sunday services. Our trained children's workers provide a safe, fun, and engaging environment.

How long are the services?

Sunday services typically last about 2 hours. Weekday services are usually 1-1.5 hours.

How do I become a member?

You can register through our E-Membership page on this website, or speak to any of our ushers during service. We also have membership classes periodically to help you learn more about our church.

Do I need to be a member to attend?

No, everyone is welcome to attend our services regardless of membership status. We encourage you to join us and experience our community.

How can I give online?

You can give online through our Give page using Flutterwave, Paystack, or bank transfer. We also accept international donations. Visit the Give page for all options.

Is my donation secure?

Yes, all online donations are processed through secure, encrypted payment platforms. Your financial information is protected.
