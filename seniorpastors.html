Meet Our Senior Pastors

Get to know our senior pastors

Learn more about the spiritual and professional journeys of our senior pastors, and how God is using them to serve in this house.

Pastor Oluyemi Olujobi

Regional Pastor & Senior Pastor

Spiritual background

She's a generous giver and believes that there is nothing one cannot give for God. She thrives on prayers and knows that everything the church and individuals do and accomplish can only be through the power of prayer.

They're building a love community such that anyone who visits the church would be met by the strong reality of God’s love and be enveloped by God’s saving grace, peace, and joy.

She’s very approachable and friendly, a good communicator with great interpersonal skills reaching out intergenerationally and inter-culturally.

She encourages the display of individual gifts and talents as she believes that every believer has a God-given ability waiting to be expressed.

Background of the Church

The church was planted in 2011 by two families The Agbezes and the Olujobi’s. In 2013, the Olujobi’s took over the main leadership responsibility and ministry work commenced in full.

The church has been through many phases and we have used many locations within Bury reaching out mainly to the Bury community those unsaved and saves, as well as everyone else in need of spiritual awakening and encouragement.

The church provides for the spiritual, physical and emotional nourishment of the people who accept the ministry and engage the community through outreach and activities.

Professional profile

Trained as a social worker and public health professional, she has worked at senior levels across corporate health, governance and strategy.
